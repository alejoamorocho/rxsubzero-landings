04 Why cold works

Six reasons your skin will thank you

One basin. A full menu of cold-powered benefits, every single morning.

  • 01

    De-puffs instantly

    Cold constricts and drains, so under-eye bags and morning swelling visibly deflate in minutes.

  • 02

    Refines pores

    A cold dip tightens the look of pores for a smoother, blurred, camera-ready surface.

  • 03

    Sculpts the jawline

    Cold firms the appearance of your contours for that snatched, lifted, defined look.

  • 04

    Cold-flushed glow

    Blood rushes back warm and bright, leaving skin flushed, dewy and lit from within.

  • 05

    Calms redness

    Soothes irritation, flushing and reactive skin for a more even, settled complexion.

  • 06

    Primes for makeup

    Tighter, cooler skin grips product beautifully — foundation sits smoother and lasts longer.
